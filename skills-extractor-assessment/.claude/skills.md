# Skill: Skill Taxonomy Mapping
- **Expertise**: High-precision mapping of IT terms to standardized skill taxonomies.
- **Task**: Extract specific "Hard Skills" (e.g., Python, AWS) and "Soft Skills" (e.g., Agile).
- **Goal**: Generate normalized skill vectors from raw Job Descriptions.
- **Constraint**: Ensure extraction is grounded in the [Skills Extractor Library](https://www.escoe.ac.uk) documentation.
